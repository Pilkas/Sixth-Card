Sixth Card

Game guide

The main goal of the game is to lay out your dealt cards and collect as little cards as possible. There are 100 cards in the deck.

To get rid off your cards you need to select a card and place it by dragging it in the middle of the table. Cards will be put in the rows automatically by these rules:

  • Chosen card is placed in the end of row in ascending order next to card with closest number
  • If there is no row with lower card, player has to choose which row to collect by clicking on it
  • If suitable row has five cards, player has to collect all cards in that row and put his card in that row (it is done automatically)

Game is played until one of the player collects 10 cards. First player who collects 10 cards - loses.
